# OpenAds — Landing Page Copy & Pitch Phrases

## Core Understanding

**OpenAds** is a self-service ad zone management platform for publishers who want to sell ad space directly on their websites — no ad networks, no middlemen. Publishers define zones, set prices, embed a widget, and get paid directly via Stripe Connect. Advertisers browse available zones and self-serve purchase campaigns.

## Feature-Driven Copy Blocks

### 1. Ad Zones

**"Define it once. Sell it forever."**
Create ad zones that match your site's layout — sidebars, banners, sponsored listings, whatever you need. Set pricing, add custom fields, and let advertisers fill in the rest.

### 2. Self-Service Campaigns

**"Let advertisers buy while you sleep."**
Advertisers pick a zone, choose their dates, fill in the details, and pay. No back-and-forth emails. No invoicing headaches. Just revenue on autopilot.

### 3. Embeddable Widget

**"One snippet. Fully integrated."**
Copy-paste a single embed code into your site. Choose your theme, layout, and dimensions. It just works — light mode, dark mode, responsive, done.

### 4. Stripe Connect Payouts

**"Get paid directly. No middleman cut."**
Payments go straight from advertiser to your Stripe account. No 30-day net terms. No mysterious deductions. You set the price, you get the price.

### 5. Custom Fields

**"Your zones, your rules."**
Every ad zone is different. Define exactly what information advertisers need to provide — links, images, descriptions, toggles. Fully customizable per zone.

### 6. Multi-Workspace

**"One account. All your sites."**
Running multiple websites? Manage them all from a single dashboard. Switch between workspaces instantly and keep everything organized.

## "How It Works" Section

1. **Create your zones** — Define where ads appear on your site and what they cost.
2. **Customize fields** — Choose what info advertisers need to provide for each zone.
3. **Embed the widget** — Drop one code snippet into your site. Pick a theme. Done.
4. **Get paid** — Advertisers self-serve purchase campaigns. Money hits your Stripe instantly.
